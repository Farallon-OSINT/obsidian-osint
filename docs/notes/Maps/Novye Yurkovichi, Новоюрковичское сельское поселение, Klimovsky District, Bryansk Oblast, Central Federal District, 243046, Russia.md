> Novy Yurkovich (Russian: Новые Юрковичи) is a rural locality (a selo) in Klimovsky District, Bryansk Oblast, Russia. It is the administrative center of [[Yurkovichskoye Rural Settlement|Yurkovichskoye Rural Settlement]] (Russian: сельское поселение Новоюрковичское).
